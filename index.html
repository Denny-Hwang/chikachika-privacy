ChikaChika Buddy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Summary: ChikaChika Buddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

ChikaChika Buddy collects minimal information to provide its core functionality:

  • Nickname (optional): A display name you choose, used only within the app to personalize brushing messages.
  • Brushing history: Records of completed brushing sessions, including date, duration, and character used.
  • App preferences: Your selected language, character, brushing duration, and volume settings.
  • Streak data: Daily brushing streak count for motivation tracking.

All of the above data is stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in storage system. We do not operate any servers or databases. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

ChikaChika Buddy does not collect:

  • Email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
  • Location data (GPS or otherwise)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other personal files
  • Health data or biometric identifiers (see Section 6 for details on camera and face data)

4. Third-Party Services

ChikaChika Buddy does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media SDKs
  • No tracking technologies of any kind

5. Children's Privacy

ChikaChika Buddy is designed to be safe and enjoyable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, no parental consent mechanism is required.

6. Camera and Face Data

ChikaChika Buddy includes a "Mirror Mode" feature that uses the device's front-facing camera to display real-time augmented reality (AR) effects while the child brushes their teeth. This section explains how camera and face data are handled.

6.1 What Face Data We Process

When Mirror Mode is active, the app uses Apple's on-device face detection APIs (via the device camera) to detect the position and basic geometry of the user's face in real-time. This includes approximate face bounding box coordinates and facial landmark positions (e.g., eyes, nose, mouth) needed to position AR effects on the screen.

6.2 How Face Data Is Used

Face data is used solely and exclusively to render AR effects (such as hats, glasses, animal ears, and other fun overlays) on the live camera preview in real-time. The purpose is to encourage children to watch themselves while brushing, which helps them brush longer and more thoroughly.

6.3 Face Data Storage and Retention

Face data is processed entirely on-device in real-time and is never stored. Each video frame is processed, used to position the AR effect, and then immediately discarded. No face data is written to disk, saved to any file, or persisted in any form. When the user leaves Mirror Mode or closes the app, all face data processing stops immediately and no face data remains.

6.4 Face Data Sharing

Face data is never transmitted, shared, or disclosed to any third party. All face detection processing occurs locally on the device using Apple's built-in frameworks. No face data is sent to any server, cloud service, analytics provider, or any other external party. The developer does not have access to any face data at any time.

6.5 Camera Permissions

Camera access requires the user's explicit permission through the standard iOS permission dialog. Camera access can be revoked at any time through the device's Settings. The app functions normally without camera access — Mirror Mode simply will not be available.

6.6 No Facial Recognition or Identification

ChikaChika Buddy does not perform facial recognition, facial identification, or any form of biometric identification. The app does not create face profiles, face templates, or faceprints. The face detection is used only to determine where to place visual AR effects on the screen.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with any third party. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no data on our end to share.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all ChikaChika Buddy data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)
  • Clearing app data through your device settings

Since no data exists on external servers, uninstalling the app ensures complete data removal.

9. Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception during transmission.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
